Graphic Designing Course

About the Course

The Graphic Designing Course offered by SMIT is an excellent opportunity for aspiring designers to unleash their creativity. This course covers essential design principles, tools, and techniques used in the graphic design industry.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Graphic Designing
  • Design Fundamentals
  • Adobe Photoshop Basics
  • Adobe Illustrator Essentials
  • Layout and Composition
  • Color Theory
  • Typography
  • Logo Design
  • Print Design
  • Web Design

Who Should Attend

This course is suitable for beginners with no prior experience in graphic designing. It is also beneficial for individuals looking to enhance their design skills and pursue a career in the creative industry.

Enrollment
