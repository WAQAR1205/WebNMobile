Saylani Mass IT Training Program

The Saylani Mass IT Training Program is a transformative initiative that provides comprehensive IT courses and workshops to individuals from diverse backgrounds. Designed by Saylani Welfare International Trust, the program aims to bridge the digital divide and empower learners with essential IT skills. With experienced instructors and hands-on learning, participants gain valuable knowledge in programming, web development, graphic design, and more. This program has made a significant impact, creating skilled professionals who secure employment, work as freelancers, or start their own ventures, contributing to a digitally literate society.

COURSES OFFERED

SMIT (Saylani Mass IT) offers a diverse and comprehensive range of six courses aimed at empowering individuals with cutting-edge IT skills. These courses cater to various interests and career aspirations. Participants can delve into the dynamic world of web and app development, equipping themselves with the ability to create interactive and user-friendly websites and applications. The graphic designing course nurtures creativity and artistic prowess, enabling students to craft visually captivating designs. The chatbot course explores the realm of artificial intelligence, empowering learners to develop intelligent conversational agents. Video animation classes bring imagination to life, teaching the art of storytelling through captivating animations. The CCNA course equips students with essential networking skills, while the AI course delves into the exciting field of Artificial Intelligence. SMIT's diverse offerings ensure that learners can find their passion and expertise in the ever-evolving landscape of information technology.
