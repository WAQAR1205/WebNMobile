Video Animation Course

About the Course

The Video Animation course at SMIT is designed to unleash your creativity and storytelling abilities through captivating animations. In this course, you will learn the fundamentals of animation principles, video editing techniques, and the usage of animation software. Whether you aspire to create engaging marketing videos or bring characters to life, this course will equip you with the skills to make your ideas come to life.

Course Highlights

  • Introduction to Animation Principles
  • Video Editing and Compositing
  • Character Animation Techniques
  • Storyboarding and Scripting
  • Hands-on Projects and Assignments
  • Industry-Experienced Instructors

Who Should Attend

This course is ideal for aspiring animators, graphic designers, marketing professionals, and anyone interested in exploring the world of video animation. Basic computer literacy is required, but no prior animation experience is necessary.

Course Duration

The Video Animation course spans 12 weeks, with classes held twice a week. Each session is 2 hours long, providing ample time for practical learning and project work.
