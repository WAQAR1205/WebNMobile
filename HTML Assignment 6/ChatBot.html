Chatbot Course

About the Course

The Chatbot Course offered by SMIT (Saylani Mass IT) is a comprehensive program designed to delve into the fascinating world of artificial intelligence and conversational agents. Participants will learn the fundamentals of chatbot development, natural language processing, and dialogue systems. The course provides hands-on experience in creating intelligent and interactive chatbots that can engage with users and provide meaningful responses. With expert instructors and practical projects, students will gain valuable skills in this rapidly evolving field.

Course Details

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Course Fee: Free (Sponsored by SMIT)
  • Prerequisites: Basic knowledge of programming and web development
  • Course Format: In-person classes and online resources

Course Curriculum

  • Introduction to Chatbots
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Dialog Management
  • Building Chatbot Interfaces
  • Machine Learning for Chatbots
  • Real-world Chatbot Projects
