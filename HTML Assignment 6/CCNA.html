CCNA Course

About the Course

The CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) course offered by Saylani Mass IT provides a comprehensive understanding of networking concepts and Cisco technologies. This course is designed to equip students with the skills required to configure, troubleshoot, and manage Cisco network devices.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Networking
  • OSI Model and TCP/IP
  • Routing and Switching Essentials
  • LAN and WAN Technologies
  • Network Security and Management
  • Hands-on Labs and Practical Projects

Prerequisites

Prior knowledge of basic networking concepts and computer operations is recommended but not mandatory. Anyone interested in building a career in networking can enroll in this course.

Course Duration

The CCNA course is typically conducted over a period of 3 months, with classes held twice a week.

Enrollment
