AI Course

Course Overview

The AI course by SMIT offers a comprehensive introduction to the fascinating world of Artificial Intelligence. Participants will learn about various AI concepts, machine learning algorithms, natural language processing, and more.

Course Details

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Mode: In-person and online
  • Prerequisites: Basic programming knowledge
  • Topics Covered: Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Deep Learning, AI Ethics, and more.

Why Choose SMIT?

SMIT is a reputable institution with years of experience in IT education. The AI course is taught by industry experts, providing valuable hands-on experience and a supportive learning environment.
