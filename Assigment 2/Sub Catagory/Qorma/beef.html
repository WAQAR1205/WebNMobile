BEEF QORMA

Beef Qorma, also known as Beef Korma, is a popular dish in Karachi, Pakistan. It features tender beef cooked in a rich and creamy sauce made with yogurt, spices, onions, garlic, ginger, and tomatoes. Beef Qorma is known for its robust and indulgent taste. It is commonly served with naan bread, roti, or rice, accompanied by raita, salad, and pickles. Karachi offers a variety of options for Beef Qorma, prepared with authentic flavors and ingredients. It is enjoyed as a satisfying and flavorful meal in the city.
