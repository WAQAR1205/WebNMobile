CHICKEN QORMA

Chicken Korma, also known as Chicken Qorma, is a popular dish in Karachi, Pakistan. It features tender chicken cooked in a rich and creamy sauce made with yogurt, spices, onions, garlic, ginger, and tomatoes. Chicken Korma is known for its indulgent and flavorful taste, with a balance of mild spices. It is commonly served with naan bread, roti, or rice, and accompanied by raita, salad, and pickles. Karachi offers a variety of options for Chicken Korma, prepared with authentic flavors and ingredients. It is enjoyed as a satisfying and delicious meal in the city.
