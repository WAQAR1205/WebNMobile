ANDA CHOLY

Anda Cholay is a popular dish in Karachi, Pakistan, consisting of boiled eggs and chickpeas in a flavorful curry. It is commonly enjoyed for breakfast or brunch and can be found at local eateries and street food stalls in Karachi. The dish features boiled eggs and tender chickpeas in a tomato-based curry seasoned with a blend of spices. Anda Cholay is typically served with naan, roti, or paratha and garnished with fresh coriander leaves. Exploring the culinary scene in Karachi allows you to discover authentic and delicious variations of this hearty and flavorful dish.
