MURGH CHOLY

Murgh Cholay is a popular chicken and chickpea curry dish enjoyed in Karachi, Pakistan. It is commonly consumed for breakfast or brunch and features tender chicken pieces and cooked chickpeas simmered in a flavorful tomato-based gravy. Murgh Cholay is known for its robust and spicy flavors, balanced by the creamy texture of the chickpeas. It is often served with naan, roti, or rice. In Karachi, you can find Murgh Cholay at local eateries and street food stalls specializing in Pakistani cuisine. Exploring the city's culinary scene allows you to experience the authentic and delicious variations of this hearty and flavorful dish.
