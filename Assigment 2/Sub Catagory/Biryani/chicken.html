CHICKEN BIRYANI

Chicken Biryani in Karachi is a popular dish that showcases the diverse culinary scene of the city. It consists of marinated chicken cooked with aromatic spices and layered with basmati rice. Karachi offers a variety of chicken biryani variations, from upscale restaurants to street food stalls. It is commonly served with raita, salad, and sometimes pickles or chutney. Chicken biryani in Karachi is enjoyed at various social gatherings and special occasions. To experience the authentic flavors, one can explore local eateries and biryani houses in the city.
