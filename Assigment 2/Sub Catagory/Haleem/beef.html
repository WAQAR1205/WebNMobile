BEEF HALEEM

Beef Haleem is a popular dish in Karachi, Pakistan, enjoyed during special occasions and particularly in the month of Ramadan. It consists of slow-cooked tender beef, lentils, wheat, and spices, resulting in a thick and creamy texture. In Karachi, Beef Haleem can be found at local eateries and restaurants specializing in Pakistani cuisine. The dish is known for its rich and flavorful taste, infused with aromatic spices like cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and nutmeg. Beef Haleem is commonly garnished with fried onions, fresh coriander leaves, ginger slices, and a squeeze of lemon. It is typically served hot with naan bread or rice, offering a satisfying and comforting meal experience.
