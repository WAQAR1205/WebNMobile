CHICKEN PALAO

Chicken Pulao is a popular rice dish in Karachi, Pakistan. It features tender chicken pieces cooked with fragrant Basmati rice and a blend of spices. Karachi offers a variety of options for Chicken Pulao, available at local eateries, restaurants, and food stalls. The chicken is marinated and simmered with spices, resulting in flavorful and tender meat. Basmati rice is added to the chicken, allowing it to absorb the flavors and aromas. Chicken Pulao is commonly garnished with fried onions, coriander leaves, and mint leaves. It is typically served with raita, salad, and sometimes accompanied by pickles or chutneys. Chicken Pulao is enjoyed during family meals, special occasions, and gatherings in Karachi as a wholesome and satisfying dish.
