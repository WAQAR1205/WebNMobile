METHA PAN

Meetha Paan, or Sweet Paan, is a popular after-meal treat in Karachi, Pakistan. It consists of a betel leaf filled with a variety of sweet ingredients such as shredded coconut, gulkand, fennel seeds, nuts, and candied fruit. Meetha Paan provides a unique and refreshing flavor experience and is commonly enjoyed as a post-meal treat or during celebrations and festivals. Karachi offers a variety of options for Meetha Paan, available at paan shops, street vendors, and sweet shops throughout the city.
