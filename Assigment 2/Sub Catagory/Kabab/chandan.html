CHANDAN KABAB

Chandan Kabab, also known as Chapli Kabab, is a popular dish in Karachi, Pakistan. It is made from minced meat mixed with a variety of spices, herbs, and ingredients such as onions, tomatoes, coriander, and green chilies. The mixture is shaped into flat round patties and cooked on a hot griddle or pan. In Karachi, you can find Chandan Kabab at local eateries, restaurants, and food stalls that specialize in Pakistani cuisine. It is commonly served with naan bread, salad, and yogurt raita. Chandan Kabab is known for its unique blend of spices, tender texture, and a slight crispiness on the outside. It is enjoyed as a main course or as a popular street food snack in Karachi.
