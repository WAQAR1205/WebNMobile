SADA CHAI

Sada Chai in Karachi is a traditional and plain tea enjoyed by locals in homes, tea stalls, and cafes. It is a staple beverage in Karachi's tea culture, known for its simplicity and comforting nature. Sada Chai is prepared by boiling tea leaves with water, milk, and sugar, resulting in a light and straightforward cup of tea. It is commonly served in small cups or glasses and is enjoyed throughout the day. Karachi's tea stalls and cafes offer an authentic experience to savor Sada Chai and immerse oneself in the local tea culture. Whether you're in a tea stall or a cozy cafe, you can easily find Sada Chai in Karachi to enjoy its traditional flavor and warmth.
