DHOODH PATTI CHAI

Dhoodh Patti Chai in Karachi is a strong and rich tea that is highly popular among locals. It is prepared using a specific type of tea leaves called "dhoodh patti" and a higher concentration of milk, resulting in a robust and full-bodied flavor. Dhoodh Patti Chai is a significant part of Karachi's tea culture and is enjoyed throughout the day in homes, tea stalls, and cafes. It is often served in small cups or glasses and is accompanied by snacks or biscuits. Karachi's tea stalls and cafes are known for their expertise in brewing Dhoodh Patti Chai to perfection, providing a delightful and energizing tea experience. Whether you're in a tea stall, cafe, or at home, you can easily find and enjoy the flavorful Dhoodh Patti Chai in Karachi.
