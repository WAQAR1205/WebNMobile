CHICKEN BOTI

Chicken Boti is a popular grilled dish enjoyed in Karachi, Pakistan. It involves marinating boneless chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and herbs before grilling them over charcoal or an open flame. Karachi offers a variety of places, from barbecue restaurants to street food stalls, where you can find delicious Chicken Boti. It is typically served with naan or paratha and accompanied by mint chutney or raita. Chicken Boti is a favorite choice for casual dining, family gatherings, and special occasions in Karachi, thanks to its juicy texture, flavorful spices, and smoky aroma. Exploring Karachi's diverse food scene allows you to discover a range of options to indulge in this delightful grilled chicken dish.
