MALAI BOTI

Malai Boti is a popular grilled dish enjoyed in Karachi, Pakistan. It features marinated chicken or beef cooked to perfection with a creamy mixture of yogurt, cream (malai), and aromatic spices. Karachi offers a variety of places, from barbecue restaurants to street food stalls, where you can find delicious Malai Boti. It is typically served with naan or paratha and accompanied by mint chutney or raita. Malai Boti is a favorite choice for casual dining and special occasions in Karachi, providing a delightful blend of flavors. Exploring the diverse food scene in Karachi allows you to experience the richness of this tasty and creamy grilled dish.
