ANDA PHARATA

Anda Paratha, or Egg Paratha, is a popular breakfast dish in Karachi, Pakistan. It features a flaky and layered paratha filled with spiced eggs. Anda Paratha is commonly found at local eateries and breakfast joints in Karachi, where it is served with chutney, yogurt, or pickle. It is a delicious and filling option enjoyed by tearing off pieces of the paratha and dipping them into the accompanying condiments.
